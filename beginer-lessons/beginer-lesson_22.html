Oila A’zolari

Oila Nima?

Oila a’zolari (family members) bizning hayotimizda muhim o‘rin tutadi. Ingliz tilida oila a’zolarining nomlarini o‘rganish muloqot qilish va o‘zaro tanishtirish uchun muhimdir.

Asosiy Oila A’zolari

Quyida ingliz tilidagi asosiy oila a’zolari va ularning tarjimalari keltirilgan:

  • Father: Ota
  • Mother: Ona
  • Son: O‘g‘il
  • Daughter: Qiz
  • Brother: Aka yoki uka
  • Sister: Opa yoki singil

Qo‘shimcha Oila A’zolari

Kengaytirilgan oila a’zolari haqida:

  • Grandfather: Bobo
  • Grandmother: Buvi
  • Uncle: Amaki yoki tog‘a
  • Aunt: Amma yoki xola
  • Cousin: Qondosh aka-uka yoki opa-singil

Oila A’zolarining Ishlatilishi

Oila a’zolari haqida gapirganda quyidagi misollarga e’tibor bering:

  • This is my father. (Bu mening otam.)
  • Her sister is very kind. (Uning singlisi juda mehribon.)
  • My grandparents live in the village. (Mening bobom va buvim qishloqda yashashadi.)

Tavsiyalar

Oila a’zolari haqida gapirishni o‘rganishda:

  • O‘z oilangizni ingliz tilida tasvirlab yozib chiqing.
  • Oila a’zolari bilan bog‘liq jumlalarni talaffuz qiling.
  • Oila a’zolari nomlarini bog‘lab qisqa matnlar tuzing.

Mashqlar: Oila A’zolari

1. Ingliz Tilida Yozing

Quyidagi oila a’zolarini ingliz tilida yozing:

  • Ota
  • Ona
  • O‘g‘il
  • Qiz

2. To‘ldirish Mashqi

Quyidagi gaplarni mos oila a’zosi nomi bilan to‘ldiring:

  • My _______ is very kind. (e.g., sister)
  • His _______ lives in the city. (e.g., uncle)
  • Her _______ is a teacher. (e.g., father)

3. Oila Daraxtini To‘ldiring

Quyidagi oila daraxtidagi bo‘sh joylarni to‘ldiring:

  • Father → _______ (e.g., mother)
  • Grandfather → _______ (e.g., grandmother)
  • Brother → _______ (e.g., sister)

4. Savollar Tuzing

Quyidagi javoblarga mos savollar yozing:

  • Answer: This is my brother. → Question: _______
  • Answer: She is my mother. → Question: _______
  • Answer: They are my grandparents. → Question: _______

5. Gaplarni To‘ldiring

Quyidagi gaplarni mos oila a’zosi bilan to‘ldiring:

  • My _______ cooks delicious meals. (mother)
  • His _______ is a doctor. (father)
  • The _______ plays football. (son)

6. Fe’llar Bilan Gap Tuzing

Quyidagi fe’llar bilan oila a’zolari haqida gaplar tuzing:

  • Live
  • Work
  • Study

7. To‘g‘ri Gapni Belgilang

Quyidagi gaplardan to‘g‘risini belgilang:

  • This is my father. / These is my father.
  • She are my mother. / She is my mother.
  • They is my grandparents. / They are my grandparents.

8. Tarjima Qiling

Quyidagi gaplarni ingliz tiliga tarjima qiling:

  • Bu mening otam.
  • U mening opam.
  • Ular mening bobom va buvim.

9. Oila A’zolarini Tasvirlang

O‘z oilangiz haqida quyidagi savollarga javob bering:

  • Who is the oldest member in your family?
  • What does your father do?
  • Do you have a brother or sister?

10. Dialog Tuzing

Quyidagi iboralardan foydalanib qisqa dialog yozing:

Who is she? / She is my sister. / What about him? / He is my brother.
