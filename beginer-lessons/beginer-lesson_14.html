Nouns (Otlar): Oddiy Narsalarning Nomlari

Nouns (Otlar) Nima?

Ingliz tilida otlar (nouns) predmetlar, joylar, odamlar, hayvonlar yoki tushunchalarning nomini bildiradi. Otlar ingliz tilidagi gapning asosiy qismidir va ular orqali predmetlarni ifodalash mumkin.

Otlarning Turlari

Ingliz tilida otlar quyidagi asosiy turlarga bo‘linadi:

  • Common nouns: umumiy predmetlarni bildiradi (e.g., table, cat, city).
  • Proper nouns: maxsus nomlar, katta harf bilan boshlanadi (e.g., London, John).
  • Abstract nouns: hissiyot va tushunchalarni bildiradi (e.g., love, happiness).
  • Collective nouns: guruhni bildiradi (e.g., team, family).

Otlarning Ishlatilishi

Ingliz tilida otlar quyidagicha ishlatiladi:

  • Subyekt: Gapda kim yoki nima haqida gap ketayotganini bildiradi (e.g., The cat is sleeping).
  • Objekt: Harakat qaysi predmetga qaratilganini bildiradi (e.g., She loves books).
  • Egalik: Otlarning egalik shakllari mavjud (e.g., John’s car).

Ko‘plik va Yakkalik

Otlarning ko‘plik shakllari odatda "-s" yoki "-es" qo‘shimchasi orqali hosil qilinadi. Maxsus otlarning o‘ziga xos ko‘plik shakllari ham bor:

  • cat → cats
  • box → boxes
  • child → children
  • man → men

Oddiy Narsalarning Nomlari

Kundalik hayotda ishlatiladigan oddiy otlar quyidagilardan iborat:

  • People: mother, teacher, friend
  • Places: park, school, shop
  • Things: chair, phone, book
  • Animals: dog, bird, elephant

Tavsiyalar

Otlarni yaxshiroq o‘zlashtirish uchun quyidagi usullardan foydalaning:

  • Kundalik hayotingizda ko‘rgan narsalarning nomlarini ingliz tilida yozib chiqing.
  • Proper nouns va common nouns farqini tushunishga harakat qiling.
  • Ko‘plik shakllarini mashq qilib yodlang.

Mashqlar: Nouns (Otlar): Oddiy Narsalarning Nomlari

1. Common va Proper Nounsni Ajrating

Quyidagi so‘zlarni common va proper nouns sifatida ajrating:

  • London
  • city
  • Sarah
  • book
  • park

2. Otlarni Belgilang

Quyidagi gaplarda otlarni topib belgilang:

  • The cat is sleeping on the sofa.
  • John went to the market.
  • She loves reading books.

3. Ko‘plikni Yozing

Quyidagi otlarning ko‘plik shaklini yozing:

  • dog
  • box
  • child
  • woman
  • pen

4. Yakkalikni Yozing

Quyidagi otlarning yakkalik shaklini yozing:

  • cats
  • buses
  • men
  • children
  • chairs

5. Otlarning Turini Aniqlang

Quyidagi otlarning turini aniqlang (common, proper, abstract, yoki collective):

  • happiness
  • team
  • teacher
  • Apple Inc.
  • family

6. Otlar bilan Gap Tuzing

Quyidagi otlarni ishlatib gap tuzing:

  • friend
  • school
  • book

7. Ob'ekt va Subyektni Ajrating

Quyidagi gaplardan subyekt va ob'ektlarni aniqlang:

  • The dog chased the ball.
  • Sarah wrote a letter.
  • The students read the book.

8. Egalikni Yozing

Quyidagi otlar bilan egalik shaklini yozing:

  • John → _______'s car
  • Sarah → _______'s book
  • The cat → _______'s toy

9. Oddiy Otlarni Toping

Quyidagi gaplardan oddiy narsalarning nomlarini toping:

  • The teacher is writing on the blackboard.
  • There is a lamp on the table.
  • We saw a dog in the park.

10. Dialog Tuzing

Quyidagi otlarni ishlatib qisqa dialog yozing:

book, school, teacher, friend
