Haftaning 7 Kuni: "Monday, Tuesday, ..."

Haftaning Kuni Nima?

Ingliz tilida haftaning 7 kuni bo‘lib, ular "Sunday"dan boshlanadi va "Saturday"da tugaydi. Bu kunlar kundalik muloqotda va jadval tuzishda muhim ahamiyatga ega.

Haftaning 7 Kuni

Quyida haftaning kunlari va ularning talaffuzi keltirilgan:

  • Monday (Dushanba) - /ˈmʌn.deɪ/
  • Tuesday (Seshanba) - /ˈtjuːz.deɪ/
  • Wednesday (Chorshanba) - /ˈwɛnz.deɪ/
  • Thursday (Payshanba) - /ˈθɜːz.deɪ/
  • Friday (Juma) - /ˈfraɪ.deɪ/
  • Saturday (Shanba) - /ˈsæt.ə.deɪ/
  • Sunday (Yakshanba) - /ˈsʌn.deɪ/

Kunlarning Talaffuzi va Ishlatilishi

Haftaning kunlarini talaffuz qilishda va ularni muloqotda ishlatishda quyidagi qoidalarga amal qilish muhim:

  • Kunlarning birinchi harfi doim katta harf bilan yoziladi: Monday, Friday.
  • Talaffuzda "Wednesday" va "Thursday" kabi so‘zlarning o‘ziga xos talaffuzi bor.
  • Kunlar bilan qo‘shimchalar ishlatishda on predlogi ishlatiladi: on Monday, on Friday.

Kunlarni Amalda Ishlatish

Haftaning kunlari quyidagi holatlarda ishlatiladi:

  • Jadval va rejalar tuzishda: My class is on Wednesday.
  • Qaysi kun bo‘lgani haqida so‘rashda: What day is it today? (Bugun qaysi kun?)
  • Kelajakdagi tadbirlarni belgilashda: We will meet on Friday.

Kunlar Bo‘yicha Tavsiyalar

Haftaning kunlarini yaxshiroq o‘zlashtirish uchun:

  • Kunlarni talaffuz qilib yodlang.
  • O‘z kunlik rejangizni ingliz tilida yozishga harakat qiling.
  • Kundalik hayotda kunlarni ishlatishga odatlaning.

Mashqlar: Haftaning 7 Kuni

1. Kunlarni Talaffuz Qiling

Quyidagi kunlarni talaffuz qilib mashq qiling:

  • Monday
  • Wednesday
  • Friday
  • Sunday

2. Kunlarni Tartiblang

Quyidagi kunlarni haftalik tartibda joylashtiring:

Friday, Monday, Sunday, Wednesday

3. Kunlarni To‘ldiring

Quyidagi gaplarni kun nomlari bilan to‘ldiring:

  • I will visit you on _______. (e.g., Monday)
  • Our meeting is on _______.
  • We play football every _______.

4. Talaffuzni Belgilang

Quyidagi kunlarning to‘g‘ri talaffuzini tanlang:

  • Wednesday → /ˈwɛnz.deɪ/ /ˈwed.nes.deɪ/
  • Thursday → /ˈθɜːz.deɪ/ /ˈthurz.day/
  • Saturday → /ˈsæt.ə.deɪ/ /ˈsat.day/

5. To‘g‘ri Kunlarni Tanlang

Quyidagi gaplar uchun to‘g‘ri kunlarni tanlang:

  • Today is the first day of the week → _______ (e.g., Sunday).
  • Tomorrow is a weekend day → _______.
  • Yesterday was a weekday → _______.

6. Kunlarni Ingliz Tilida Yozing

Quyidagi kunlarni ingliz tilida yozing:

  • Dushanba
  • Seshanba
  • Payshanba
  • Yakshanba

7. Kunlarni Gaplarda Ishlating

Quyidagi gaplarga mos kunlarni yozing:

  • My favorite day is _______. (e.g., Friday)
  • I don’t have school on _______.
  • We always have a meeting on _______.

8. Kunlarni Tartibda Belgilang

Quyidagi gaplarga mos keladigan kunni yozing:

  • The second day of the week → _______
  • The last day of the week → _______
  • The fourth day of the week → _______

9. Kunlarga Predlog Qo‘shing

Quyidagi gaplarga mos predlogni qo‘shib yozing:

  • I will meet you _______ Monday. (e.g., on)
  • Our holiday starts _______ Friday.
  • We don’t work _______ Saturday.

10. Dialog Yozing

Quyidagi iboralar asosida qisqa dialog tuzing:

What day is it today? / It’s Monday. / What about tomorrow?
