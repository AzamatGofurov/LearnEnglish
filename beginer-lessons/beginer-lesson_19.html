Exercises: Identify by Color or Shape

1. Identify the Color

Write the colors of the following items in English:

  • Green leaf
  • Red apple
  • Blue sky

2. Identify the Shape

Write the shapes of the following items in English:

  • Round ball
  • Square windows
  • Triangular pizza

3. Fill in the Blanks

Complete the sentences with the appropriate color or shape:

  • The flag is _______. (red and white)
  • The gift box is _______. (square)
  • The ball is _______. (round)

4. Match Color and Shape

Match the colors and shapes of the following items:

  • Ball → _______ (round, red)
  • Book → _______ (rectangular, blue)
  • Pizza slice → _______ (triangular, yellow)

5. Describe Items

Describe the following items by their color and shape:

  • Clock
  • Table
  • Star

6. Separate Colors and Shapes

Sort the following words into colors and shapes:

  • Red, Circle, Green, Square, Yellow, Triangle

7. Match Items with Colors and Shapes

Fill in the blanks with the correct color and shape:

  • The plate is _______ and _______. (circle, white)
  • The box is _______ and _______. (square, brown)
  • The flag is _______ and _______. (rectangle, green)

8. Identify Colors and Shapes

Find and write the colors or shapes from the following sentences:

  • The sun is yellow and round.
  • The road sign is triangular and red.
  • The kite is colorful and square.

9. Use Colors and Shapes in a Dialog

Write a short dialog using the following phrases:

What shape is the box? / It’s square. / What color is it? / It’s blue.

10. Create Sentences with Colors and Shapes

Write sentences about the following items using their colors and shapes:

  • Apple
  • Clock
  • Table
