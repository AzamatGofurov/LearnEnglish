Personal Information: "My name is..."

Introducing Personal Information

Introducing personal information in English is a fundamental skill for starting conversations and interacting with new people. The simplest phrase for introductions is:

My name is...

This phrase can be used in both formal and informal settings. Examples:

  • My name is John.
  • I am Sarah.
  • You can call me Alex.

Formal Introductions

In formal situations, use the following phrases to introduce yourself:

  • Let me introduce myself.
  • My full name is...
  • I work as a...

Informal Introductions

In informal situations, simple phrases are used for introductions:

  • I’m...
  • Call me...
  • Hi, I’m...

Examples:

  • Hi, I’m Emily.
  • You can call me Tom.

Adding Personal Information

To share more about yourself, use the following phrases:

  • I’m from...
  • I live in...
  • I’m a student.
  • I like...

Tips for Learning

The following methods can help you master introducing personal information:

  • Memorize simple and concise phrases.
  • Practice saying your personal information out loud.
  • Use these phrases when interacting with new people.

Exercises: "My name is..."

1. Complete the Sentences

Fill in the missing words in the sentences below:

  • My name _______ John. (is)
  • You can _______ me Alex. (call)
  • Let me _______ myself. (introduce)

2. Identify Formal or Informal

Mark whether the following phrases are formal or informal:

  • My name is Sarah.
  • Call me Emily.
  • Let me introduce myself.

3. Provide Answers

Write suitable answers for the questions below:

  • What’s your name? → _______
  • Where are you from? → _______
  • What do you do? → _______

4. Complete the Introduction

Fill in the blanks to complete the introduction phrases:

  • My name is _______. I’m from _______. (e.g., Emily, New York)
  • I live in _______ and I work as a _______. (e.g., London, teacher)

5. Choose the Correct Phrase

Select the appropriate phrase for each situation:

  • At a formal meeting → My name is... / Hi, I’m...
  • Talking to a friend → Let me introduce myself. / Call me...

6. Add Personal Information

Complete the phrases below with your information:

  • My name is _______. I like _______. (e.g., Sarah, playing football)
  • I’m from _______. I live in _______. (e.g., Uzbekistan, Tashkent)

7. Answer the Questions

Write your answers to the following questions:

  • What’s your full name?
  • Where do you live?
  • What do you like to do?

8. Arrange in Order

Place the following phrases in the correct order for an introduction:

I live in London. / My name is John. / I’m a teacher.

9. Create a Dialogue

Use the phrases below to create a short dialogue:

My name is..., I’m from..., I like...

10. Fill in the Words

Complete the missing words in the sentences below:

  • Hi, _______ name is Alex. (my)
  • I _______ in New York. (live)
  • You _______ call me Sam. (can)
