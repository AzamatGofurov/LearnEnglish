Oddiy Gaplarda Ishlatish

Oddiy Gap Nima?

Oddiy gap (simple sentence) bitta subyekt (kim yoki nima haqida gap ketayotganini bildiruvchi) va bitta predikatdan (harakat yoki holatni bildiruvchi) iborat bo‘lgan gapdir. U qisqa va sodda tuzilgan bo‘ladi.

  • Misol: She runs. (U yuguradi.)
  • Misol: I eat an apple. (Men olma yeyman.)

Oddiy Gapning Tuzilishi

Oddiy gaplarning tuzilishi quyidagicha bo‘ladi:

  • Subject + Verb: The cat sleeps. (Mushuk uxlaydi.)
  • Subject + Verb + Object: He reads a book. (U kitob o‘qiydi.)
  • Subject + Verb + Adjective: The sky is blue. (Osmon moviy.)
  • Subject + Verb + Adverb: She walks quickly. (U tez yuradi.)

Fe’llarning Rolini Anglash

Oddiy gaplarda fe’llar harakatni yoki holatni ifodalaydi. Fe’l gapning markaziy qismi bo‘lib, uning ma’nosini shakllantiradi.

  • Harakat: The dog runs. (It yuguradi.)
  • Holat: She is happy. (U baxtli.)

Fe’llarni Turlanishi

Oddiy gaplarda fe’llar subyekt va zamonga qarab o‘zgaradi. Misollar:

  • Present Simple: I play football. (Men futbol o‘ynayman.)
  • Past Simple: I played football. (Men futbol o‘ynadim.)
  • Future Simple: I will play football. (Men futbol o‘ynayman.)

Oddiy Gaplarni Ishlatish

Oddiy gaplar quyidagi holatlarda ishlatiladi:

  • Harakatni tasvirlash: He writes a letter. (U maktub yozadi.)
  • Holatni tasvirlash: The room is clean. (Xona toza.)
  • Savol berishda: Do you like apples? (Siz olmani yoqtirasizmi?)
  • Javob berishda: Yes, I do. (Ha, yoqtiraman.)

Tavsiyalar

Oddiy gaplarni mustahkamlash uchun:

  • Fe’l va subyektlarni o‘rganing.
  • Oddiy gaplar bilan jumlalar tuzib mashq qiling.
  • Gapning tuzilishini yaxshi tushunish uchun kundalik hayotdagi holatlarni tasvirlang.

Mashqlar: Oddiy Gaplarda Ishlatish

1. To‘g‘ri Fe’lni Tanlang

Quyidagi gaplarni mos fe’l bilan to‘ldiring:

  • I _______ every day. (walk / walks)
  • She _______ tea every morning. (drink / drinks)
  • We _______ football yesterday. (play / played)

2. Gaplarni To‘ldiring

Quyidagi gaplarni mos so‘zlar bilan to‘ldiring:

  • The sky is _______. (blue)
  • He writes a _______. (letter)
  • They walk to _______. (school)

3. Gaplarni Tuzing

Quyidagi so‘zlar bilan to‘g‘ri gaplar tuzing:

  • She / to eat / apple / an
  • We / to play / football
  • The cat / to sleep / on the bed

4. Gaplarni O‘zgartiring

Quyidagi gaplarni o‘tgan zamonga o‘zgartiring:

  • He writes a letter. → _______
  • We play in the park. → _______
  • She reads a book. → _______

5. Savol Yozing

Quyidagi gaplarga mos savollar tuzing:

  • She drinks coffee every morning.
  • They play football in the evening.
  • He writes letters to his friends.

6. Gaplarni Tartiblang

Quyidagi gaplarni so‘zlarni to‘g‘ri tartibda joylashtiring:

  • to / she / the park / goes
  • on / the cat / the bed / sleeps
  • apple / I / eat / an

7. Fe’lni Toping

Quyidagi gaplardan fe’llarni topib yozing:

  • The dog runs in the garden.
  • I read books in my free time.
  • She is happy today.

8. Gaplarni To‘g‘ri Shaklga O‘zgartiring

Quyidagi gaplarni savol yoki salbiy gaplarga o‘zgartiring:

  • He eats breakfast. → Savol shakli: _______
  • They play football. → Salbiy shakli: _______
  • She reads a book. → Savol shakli: _______

9. Fe’lga Mos Gap Tuzing

Quyidagi fe’llar bilan oddiy gaplar yozing:

  • Run
  • Write
  • Play

10. Dialog Tuzing

Quyidagi iboralardan foydalanib qisqa dialog yozing:

What do you do in the morning? / I drink coffee. / Do you go to the park?
