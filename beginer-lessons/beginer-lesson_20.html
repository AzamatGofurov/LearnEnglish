Fe’llar va Ularning Ishlatilishi

Fe’llar Nima?

Fe’llar (verbs) ingliz tilidagi gaplarning asosiy qismidir. Ular harakatni, holatni yoki vaziyatni bildiradi. Masalan:

  • to run (yugurmoq)
  • to eat (ovqatlanmoq)
  • to be (bo‘lmoq)

Fe’llarning Turlari

Ingliz tilida fe’llar turli xil vazifalarni bajaradi. Ular quyidagi asosiy turlarga bo‘linadi:

  • Action verbs: Harakatni bildiradi (e.g., to run, to jump, to eat).
  • Linking verbs: Holatni bildiradi va subyektni aniqlaydi (e.g., to be, to seem, to appear).
  • Helping verbs: Asosiy fe’lga qo‘shimcha ma’no beradi (e.g., to have, to do, to will).

Fe’llarning Ishlatilishi

Fe’llar turli xil holatlarda ishlatiladi:

  • Harakatni ifodalash: I run every morning. (Men har kuni ertalab yuguraman.)
  • Holatni ifodalash: She is happy. (U baxtli.)
  • Yordamchi so‘z sifatida: He has finished his homework. (U uy vazifasini tugatgan.)

Fe’llarning Zamonlarga Ko‘ra O‘zgarishi

Fe’llar zamonga qarab o‘z shaklini o‘zgartiradi. Quyidagi misollarga e’tibor bering:

  • Hozirgi zamon: I eat. (Men ovqatlanaman.)
  • O‘tgan zamon: I ate. (Men ovqatlandim.)
  • Kelasi zamon: I will eat. (Men ovqatlanaman.)

Noto‘g‘ri Fe’llar

Ingliz tilida ba’zi fe’llar odatdagi qo‘shimcha (-ed) yordamida o‘tgan zamonga o‘zgarmaydi. Ular noto‘g‘ri fe’llar deb ataladi:

  • Go → Went → Gone
  • Eat → Ate → Eaten
  • See → Saw → Seen

Tavsiyalar

Fe’llarni yaxshiroq o‘zlashtirish uchun:

  • Harakat fe’llari bilan oddiy gaplar tuzing va talaffuz qiling.
  • Linking va yordamchi fe’llarning qo‘llanilishini mashq qiling.
  • Noto‘g‘ri fe’llar ro‘yxatini yodlab, ularni mashqlarda qo‘llang.

Mashqlar: Fe’llar va Ularning Ishlatilishi

1. To‘g‘ri Fe’lni Tanlang

Quyidagi gaplarni mos fe’l bilan to‘ldiring:

  • I _______ every morning. (run / eats)
  • She _______ a book now. (reads / read)
  • They _______ to the park yesterday. (went / go)

2. Fe’lni Zamonga Moslang

Quyidagi fe’llarni mos zamonga moslashtirib yozing:

  • (Hozirgi zamon) He _______ (to play).
  • (O‘tgan zamon) We _______ (to watch) TV.
  • (Kelasi zamon) I _______ (to visit) my friend tomorrow.

3. Noto‘g‘ri Fe’llarni Yozing

Quyidagi noto‘g‘ri fe’llarning hozirgi, o‘tgan va o‘tgan zamon (PP) shakllarini yozing:

  • Go → _______ → _______
  • Eat → _______ → _______
  • See → _______ → _______

4. Fe’l Turlarini Ajrating

Quyidagi fe’llarni harakat (action), bog‘lovchi (linking), yoki yordamchi (helping) sifatida ajrating:

  • Run
  • Is
  • Have
  • Seem
  • Write

5. Gaplarni Tugating

Quyidagi gaplarni mos fe’llar bilan tugating:

  • She _______ to school every day. (walk)
  • They _______ a movie last night. (watch)
  • We _______ a picnic tomorrow. (have)

6. Fe’lni Gapga Joylashtiring

Quyidagi so‘zlar bilan to‘g‘ri gaplar tuzing:

  • I / to eat / breakfast / morning / every
  • She / book / reading / is
  • They / yesterday / to the park / went

7. Fe’lni Moslang

Quyidagi gaplarni o‘tgan zamonga o‘zgartiring:

  • She writes a letter. → _______
  • We play football. → _______
  • He goes to the store. → _______

8. Fe’llarni Belgilang

Quyidagi gaplardan fe’llarni topib, ularning turlarini yozing:

  • They are playing football.
  • She seems tired.
  • I have finished my homework.

9. Fe’l Bilan Dialog Tuzing

Quyidagi iboralarni ishlatib qisqa dialog yozing:

What are you doing? / I’m reading a book. / Did you read yesterday?

10. Fe’l Bilan Gaplarni Tuzing

Quyidagi fe’llar bilan gaplar tuzing:

  • Run
  • Eat
  • Write
