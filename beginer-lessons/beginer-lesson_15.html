Articles (A, An, The): Qachon va Qanday Ishlatiladi

Articles Nima?

Ingliz tilida "articles" (artikl) otlarni aniqlash yoki chegaralash uchun ishlatiladi. Uch turdagi artikl mavjud:

  • A: birinchi marta aytilayotgan, umumiy va unli tovush bilan boshlanmagan otlar uchun.
  • An: birinchi marta aytilayotgan, umumiy va unli tovush bilan boshlanuvchi otlar uchun.
  • The: ma’lum va aniq otlar uchun.

A va An Artikllari

A va An artikllari birlikdagi umumiy otlarni bildiradi va ularning birinchi tovushiga qarab tanlanadi:

  • A: Unli tovush bilan boshlanmagan otlar uchun (e.g., a cat, a book).
  • An: Unli tovush bilan boshlanuvchi otlar uchun (e.g., an apple, an hour).

Misollar:

  • I saw a dog in the park.
  • She is eating an orange.
  • It took me an hour to finish.

The Artikli

The artikli ma’lum yoki gapda oldin tilga olingan otlarni aniqlash uchun ishlatiladi:

  • Bizga ma’lum bir narsa haqida gap ketayotganida: The book on the table is mine.
  • Dunyoda yagona bo‘lgan narsa haqida gapirilganda: The sun is shining.
  • Odatda ma’lum guruh yoki joyni aniqlash uchun: The students are in the classroom.

Articles Ishlatilmaydigan Holatlar

Ba’zi holatlarda artikllar ishlatilmaydi:

  • Ko‘plikdagi umumiy otlar: Dogs are friendly.
  • Material yoki umumiy tushunchalar: Water is essential.
  • Tushunchaviy otlar: Happiness is important.

Tavsiyalar

Artikllarni yaxshi o‘zlashtirish uchun:

  • "A" va "An" qoidalarini unli va undosh tovushlar asosida yod oling.
  • "The" artiklini ishlatishda ma’lum va noma’lum otlarni farqlashga e’tibor bering.
  • Artikllarni inglizcha kitob o‘qib yoki video tomosha qilib mashq qiling.

Mashqlar: Articles (A, An, The)

1. To‘g‘ri Artiklni Tanlang

Quyidagi jumlalarni mos artikl bilan to‘ldiring:

  • I saw _______ cat in the garden. (a / an / the)
  • She is eating _______ apple. (a / an / the)
  • _______ moon is very bright tonight. (a / an / the)

2. Artiklni Ishlating

Quyidagi gaplarni to‘ldiring:

  • There is _______ book on the table. (a / an / the)
  • She bought _______ umbrella because it was raining. (a / an / the)
  • We visited _______ Eiffel Tower last summer. (a / an / the)

3. Artikllarni Belgilang

Quyidagi jumlalarda ishlatilgan artikllarni belgilang:

  • I saw an elephant at the zoo.
  • We have a meeting at the office.
  • The sun is shining brightly.

4. Artiklni Ishlatish Holatlarini Tushuning

Quyidagi gaplarda artikllar ishlatilishi yoki ishlatilmasligini tushuntiring:

  • Water is essential for life.
  • She has a pet dog.
  • Mount Everest is the tallest mountain.

5. Artiklsiz Gaplarni To‘ldiring

Quyidagi gaplarga mos artikllarni qo‘shing:

  • There is _______ orange on the table.
  • He found _______ key on the floor.
  • They are watching _______ movie tonight.

6. Artiklni Farqlang

Quyidagi otlarga mos artiklni yozing:

  • _______ hour
  • _______ dog
  • _______ university

7. Artikllarni Toping

Quyidagi jumlalardan artikllarni topib belgilab chiqing:

  • The children are playing in the park.
  • I need an envelope to send this letter.
  • A car is parked outside our house.

8. Artikllar Qoidasi

Quyidagi gaplarning qaysi biri to‘g‘ri ekanligini belgilang:

  • I saw a hour on the clock. / I saw an hour on the clock.
  • He is an honest man. / He is a honest man.
  • The sun rises in the east. / A sun rises in the east.

9. Artikllar bilan Gap Tuzing

Quyidagi otlarni ishlatib gaplar tuzing:

  • apple
  • house
  • ocean

10. Artikllar bilan Dialog Tuzing

Quyidagi iboralardan foydalanib qisqa dialog tuzing:

Do you have a pen? / Yes, I have an extra one. / Where is the pen you gave me?
