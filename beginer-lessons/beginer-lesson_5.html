Hol-ahvol So‘rash va Javob Berish

Hol-ahvol So‘rashning Muhimligi

Hol-ahvol so‘rash — muloqotni boshlash va suhbatni davom ettirishning muhim qismidir. Ingliz tilida hol-ahvol so‘rash va unga javob berishning bir necha turli usullari mavjud. Bu iboralar rasmiy va norasmiy holatlarda turlicha bo‘lishi mumkin.

Rasmiy Hol-ahvol So‘rash

Quyidagi iboralar rasmiy holatlarda ishlatiladi:

  • How are you? (Qandaysiz?)
  • How do you do? (Sizni ko‘rib xursandman.)
  • Are you doing well? (Yaxshi yuribsizmi?)

Rasmiy javoblar:

  • I’m fine, thank you. And you? (Yaxshi, rahmat. Siz-chi?)
  • Very well, thank you. (Juda yaxshi, rahmat.)

Norasmiy Hol-ahvol So‘rash

Quyidagi iboralar norasmiy holatlarda ishlatiladi:

  • What’s up? (Nima gaplar?)
  • How’s it going? (Qanday?)
  • How are things? (Ishlar qalay?)

Norasmiy javoblar:

  • Not bad. (Yomon emas.)
  • Pretty good. (Juda yaxshi.)
  • All right. (Hammasi joyida.)

Hol-ahvolga Javob Berishda Xushmuomalalik

Hol-ahvol so‘rashga javob berayotganda xushmuomalalikni unutmaslik kerak. Bu suhbatdoshga hurmatni ko‘rsatadi va muloqotni iliqlashtiradi. Misollar:

  • I’m fine, thank you. And you?
  • Pretty good, how about you?
  • Not bad, thanks for asking.

Hol-ahvol So‘rash Bo‘yicha Tavsiyalar

Quyidagi tavsiyalar hol-ahvol so‘rash va javob berishni yaxshiroq o‘zlashtirishga yordam beradi:

  • Turli holatlar uchun mos iboralarni mashq qiling.
  • Do‘stlaringiz yoki o‘rganish sheriklaringiz bilan amaliyot o‘tkazing.
  • Audio yoki video materiallardan foydalanib, talaffuzni o‘rganing.

Mashqlar: Hol-ahvol So‘rash va Javob Berish

1. Iboralarni To‘ldiring

Quyidagi iboralardagi tushib qolgan so‘zlarni yozing:

  • How _______ you? (are)
  • What’s _______? (up)
  • How _______ it going? (is)

2. Rasmiy yoki Norasmiyligini Belgilang

Quyidagi iboralar rasmiy yoki norasmiy ekanligini belgilang:

  • How do you do?
  • What’s up?
  • Are you doing well?

3. Javoblarni Toping

Quyidagi iboralarga mos javoblarni yozing:

  • How are you? → _______
  • What’s up? → _______
  • How’s it going? → _______

4. To‘g‘ri Iborani Tanlang

Quyidagi holatlar uchun to‘g‘ri iborani tanlang:

  • Rasmiy uchrashuvda → _______
  • Do‘st bilan suhbatda → _______
  • O‘qituvchi bilan muloqotda → _______

5. Javobni To‘ldiring

Quyidagi iboralarga mos ravishda to‘liq javob yozing:

  • I’m ________, thank you. And you?
  • Pretty ________, how about you?
  • Not ________, thanks for asking.

6. Hol-ahvol So‘rashni Amalda Qo‘llash

Quyidagi iboralardan foydalanib dialog tuzing:

How are you? I’m fine, thank you. And you?

7. To‘g‘ri Javobni Toping

Quyidagi iboralardan mos javobni tanlang:

  • What’s up? → Not bad / Good morning
  • How are things? → I’m fine / Pretty good
  • Are you doing well? → Very well, thank you / Hey!

8. Qaysi Iborani Ishlatish Kerak?

Quyidagi holatlar uchun to‘g‘ri iborani tanlang:

  • Yangi tanishgan kishi bilan → _______
  • Ko‘pdan beri ko‘rmagan do‘st bilan → _______
  • Oila a’zosi bilan → _______

9. Talaffuzni Mashq Qiling

Quyidagi iboralarni talaffuz qilib mashq qiling:

How are you?, What’s up?, Are you doing well?

10. Hol-ahvol So‘rashning Mantiqiy Tartibi

Quyidagi iboralarni to‘g‘ri tartibda joylashtiring:

Hello, How are you?, I’m fine, thank you. And you?
