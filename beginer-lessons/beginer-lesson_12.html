The 12 Months of the Year

In English, there are 12 months of the year, arranged as follows. Each month's name starts with a capital letter and has a unique pronunciation.

  • January (Yanvar) - /ˈdʒæn.jʊ.ər.i/
  • February (Fevral) - /ˈfɛb.ruː.ər.i/
  • March (Mart) - /mɑːrtʃ/
  • April (Aprel) - /ˈeɪ.prɪl/
  • May (May) - /meɪ/
  • June (Iyun) - /dʒuːn/
  • July (Iyul) - /dʒʊˈlaɪ/
  • August (Avgust) - /ˈɔː.gəst/
  • September (Sentabr) - /sɛpˈtɛm.bər/
  • October (Oktabr) - /ɒkˈtoʊ.bər/
  • November (Noyabr) - /noʊˈvɛm.bər/
  • December (Dekabr) - /dɪˈsɛm.bər/

Pronunciation and Usage of Months

In English, there are important rules for pronouncing and using months in sentences:

  • Month names are written with a capital letter: January, March.
  • The preposition in is used with month names: in January, in December.
  • When numbers are used before months, the preposition of is added: 10th of March.

Practical Uses of Months

Months are commonly used in the following contexts:

  • To mention birthdays and important dates: I was born in April.
  • For planning: We will go on vacation in July.
  • To discuss weather: It’s very cold in December.

Months Linked to Seasons

In English, months are also associated with seasons as follows:

  • Winter: December, January, February
  • Spring: March, April, May
  • Summer: June, July, August
  • Autumn (Fall): September, October, November

Tips

To master the names of months, try the following methods:

  • Memorize the pronunciation of the months.
  • Write your birthday or important dates in English.
  • Use month names when planning your daily schedules.

Exercises: The 12 Months of the Year

1. Write the Months in English

Write the names of the following months in English:

  • Yanvar
  • Mart
  • May
  • Iyul
  • Oktyabr

2. Practice Pronunciation of the Months

Practice pronouncing the following months:

  • February
  • April
  • August
  • December

3. Categorize the Months by Seasons

Sort the following months into seasons:

  • March, June, October, January

4. Fill in the Months

Complete the following sentences with month names:

  • I was born in _______. (e.g., June)
  • My favorite month is _______.
  • We always celebrate New Year in _______.

5. Arrange the Months

Place the following months in chronological order:

May, January, December, March

6. Choose the Correct Preposition

Add the correct preposition to the following sentences:

  • We will travel _______ July. (e.g., in)
  • The event is _______ 5th of February.
  • My exam is _______ September.

7. Identify the Correct Months

Write the months that match the following descriptions:

  • The first month of the year → _______
  • The last month of the year → _______
  • The month after April → _______

8. Match the Month to the Season

Write the months that correspond to these seasons:

  • Winter → _______
  • Summer → _______
  • Autumn → _______

9. Complete the Sentences

Fill in the blanks in these sentences with appropriate months:

  • The hottest month in my country is _______. (e.g., July)
  • The coldest month in my country is _______.
  • The flowers bloom in _______.

10. Create a Dialogue

Write a short dialogue using the following prompts:

What month is it now? / It’s November. / When is your birthday?
