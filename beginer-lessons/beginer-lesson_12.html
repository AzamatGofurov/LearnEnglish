Yilning 12 Oyi: "January, February, ..."

Yilning 12 Oyi

Ingliz tilida yilning 12 oyi mavjud bo‘lib, ular quyidagicha tartiblanadi. Har bir oyning nomi katta harf bilan boshlanadi va talaffuzi o‘ziga xosdir.

  • January (Yanvar) - /ˈdʒæn.jʊ.ər.i/
  • February (Fevral) - /ˈfɛb.ruː.ər.i/
  • March (Mart) - /mɑːrtʃ/
  • April (Aprel) - /ˈeɪ.prɪl/
  • May (May) - /meɪ/
  • June (Iyun) - /dʒuːn/
  • July (Iyul) - /dʒʊˈlaɪ/
  • August (Avgust) - /ˈɔː.gəst/
  • September (Sentabr) - /sɛpˈtɛm.bər/
  • October (Oktabr) - /ɒkˈtoʊ.bər/
  • November (Noyabr) - /noʊˈvɛm.bər/
  • December (Dekabr) - /dɪˈsɛm.bər/

Oylarning Talaffuzi va Ishlatilishi

Ingliz tilida oylarning talaffuzi va ularni gaplarda ishlatishning muhim qoidalari mavjud:

  • Oy nomlari katta harf bilan yoziladi: January, March.
  • Oy nomlari bilan birga in predlogi ishlatiladi: in January, in December.
  • Oylardan oldin raqamlar ishlatilganda of qo‘llaniladi: 10th of March.

Oylarning Amalda Qo‘llanilishi

Oylardan foydalanish quyidagi holatlarda keng tarqalgan:

  • Tug‘ilgan kunlar va muhim sanalarni aytishda: I was born in April.
  • Reja tuzishda: We will go on vacation in July.
  • Ob-havo haqida gaplashishda: It’s very cold in December.

Oylarning Fasllar Bilan Bog‘lanishi

Ingliz tilida oylardan tashqari fasllar ham mavjud va ular quyidagi tartibda bog‘lanadi:

  • Winter: December, January, February
  • Spring: March, April, May
  • Summer: June, July, August
  • Autumn (Fall): September, October, November

Tavsiyalar

Oylarning nomlarini o‘zlashtirish uchun quyidagi usullardan foydalaning:

  • Oylarning talaffuzini yodlang.
  • O‘zingizning tug‘ilgan kuningiz yoki muhim sanalarni ingliz tilida yozing.
  • Kunlik rejalar tuzayotganda oy nomlarini ishlatishga harakat qiling.

Mashqlar: Yilning 12 Oyi

1. Oylarni Ingliz Tilida Yozing

Quyidagi oylarning nomlarini ingliz tilida yozing:

  • Yanvar
  • Mart
  • May
  • Iyul
  • Oktyabr

2. Oylarning Talaffuzini Mashq Qiling

Quyidagi oylarning talaffuzini mashq qiling:

  • February
  • April
  • August
  • December

3. Oylarni Fasllarga Ajrating

Quyidagi oylarni fasllarga ajrating:

  • March, June, October, January

4. Oylarni To‘ldiring

Quyidagi gaplarni oy nomlari bilan to‘ldiring:

  • I was born in _______. (e.g., June)
  • My favorite month is _______.
  • We always celebrate New Year in _______.

5. Oylarni Tartiblang

Quyidagi oylarni tartib bo‘yicha joylashtiring:

May, January, December, March

6. To‘g‘ri Predlogni Tanlang

Quyidagi gaplarga mos predlogni qo‘shing:

  • We will travel _______ July. (e.g., in)
  • The event is _______ 5th of February.
  • My exam is _______ September.

7. To‘g‘ri Oylarni Toping

Quyidagi gaplarga mos oylarni yozing:

  • The first month of the year → _______
  • The last month of the year → _______
  • The month after April → _______

8. Faslga Mos Oyni Toping

Quyidagi fasllarga mos oylarni yozing:

  • Winter → _______
  • Summer → _______
  • Autumn → _______

9. Gaplarni To‘ldiring

Quyidagi gaplarni mos oylar bilan to‘ldiring:

  • The hottest month in my country is _______. (e.g., July)
  • The coldest month in my country is _______.
  • The flowers bloom in _______.

10. Dialog Tuzing

Quyidagi iboralardan foydalanib qisqa dialog yozing:

What month is it now? / It’s November. / When is your birthday?
