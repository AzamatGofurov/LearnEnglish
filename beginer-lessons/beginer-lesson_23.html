Ovqat va Ichimliklar

Ovqat va Ichimliklar Nima?

Ovqat va ichimliklar hayotimizning muhim qismi bo‘lib, ular haqidagi so‘zlarni ingliz tilida bilish kundalik suhbatlarda, restoranlarda va ovqat buyurtma qilishda yordam beradi.

Asosiy Ovqatlar

Quyida ingliz tilida keng qo‘llaniladigan asosiy ovqat nomlari keltirilgan:

  • Bread: Non
  • Rice: Guruch
  • Pasta: Makaron
  • Meat: Go‘sht
  • Fish: Baliq
  • Eggs: Tuxum
  • Vegetables: Sabzavotlar
  • Fruits: Mevalar

Asosiy Ichimliklar

Quyida asosiy ichimliklarning nomlari keltirilgan:

  • Water: Suv
  • Tea: Choy
  • Coffee: Qahva
  • Milk: Sut
  • Juice: Sharbat
  • Soda: Gazlangan ichimlik

Ovqat va Ichimliklar Ishlatilishi

Ovqat va ichimliklar haqida gapirganda quyidagi misollarga e’tibor bering:

  • I like bread and butter. (Men non va yog‘ni yoqtiraman.)
  • She drinks coffee every morning. (U har kuni ertalab qahva ichadi.)
  • We eat rice and vegetables for dinner. (Biz kechki ovqatga guruch va sabzavotlar yeymiz.)

Tavsiyalar

Ovqat va ichimliklar so‘zlarini yaxshiroq o‘rganish uchun:

  • Kundalik ovqatlanish ratsioningizdagi mahsulotlarni ingliz tilida nomlang.
  • Restoran menyularini ingliz tilida o‘qing va tushuning.
  • Ovqat va ichimliklarga bog‘liq jumlalarni talaffuz qiling va yodlang.

Mashqlar: Ovqat va Ichimliklar

1. Ingliz Tilida Yozing

Quyidagi ovqat va ichimliklarning nomlarini ingliz tilida yozing:

  • Non
  • Guruch
  • Suv
  • Choy

2. To‘g‘ri Nomni Belgilang

Quyidagi mahsulotlarga mos keladigan to‘g‘ri nomni tanlang:

  • Bread / Rice / Milk → Non
  • Fish / Meat / Juice → Baliq
  • Tea / Coffee / Soda → Choy

3. Ovqatlarni Tasniflang

Quyidagi mahsulotlarni ovqat va ichimliklarga ajrating:

  • Water, Bread, Rice, Juice, Vegetables

4. Gaplarni To‘ldiring

Quyidagi gaplarni mos so‘zlar bilan to‘ldiring:

  • I drink _______ every morning. (tea)
  • She eats _______ for breakfast. (bread)
  • We like _______ and _______ for dinner. (rice, vegetables)

5. Savolga Javob Bering

Quyidagi savollarga javob bering:

  • What do you drink in the morning?
  • What is your favorite food?
  • Do you like coffee or tea?

6. Mos Keladigan Gapni Tanlang

Quyidagi gaplardan to‘g‘risini tanlang:

  • I eat water. / I drink water.
  • She likes meat. / She likes juice.
  • We eat rice and fish. / We drink bread.

7. Ovqat va Ichimliklar Bilan Gap Tuzing

Quyidagi mahsulotlar bilan gaplar tuzing:

  • Milk
  • Fish
  • Vegetables

8. Tarjima Qiling

Quyidagi gaplarni ingliz tiliga tarjima qiling:

  • Men har kuni guruch yeyman.
  • U har kuni ertalab qahva ichadi.
  • Biz kechki ovqatga sabzavotlar yeymiz.

9. Savollar Tuzing

Quyidagi javoblarga mos savollar yozing:

  • Answer: I drink tea in the morning. → Question: _______
  • Answer: My favorite food is pizza. → Question: _______
  • Answer: Yes, I like coffee. → Question: _______

10. Dialog Tuzing

Quyidagi iboralardan foydalanib qisqa dialog yozing:

What do you eat for dinner? / I eat rice and vegetables. / Do you drink water? / Yes, I do.
