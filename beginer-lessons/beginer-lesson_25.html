"What is your name?"

"What is your name?" Nima Ma'noni Anglatadi?

"What is your name?" (Sizning ismingiz nima?) — bu odatda tanishish jarayonida ishlatiladigan oddiy va muhim savol. Ushbu savolga javob berish orqali o‘zingizni tanishtirishingiz mumkin.

Savolning Tuzilishi

"What is your name?" savoli quyidagicha tuziladi:

  • What: Nima?
  • is: To be fe’lining hozirgi zamondagi shakli (birlikda ishlatiladi).
  • your: Sizning.
  • name: Ism.

Savol butunlay yig‘ilganda: "What is your name?" → Sizning ismingiz nima?

Javob Berish

Ushbu savolga javob berishda quyidagi formuladan foydalaniladi:

  • My name is [ism]. (Mening ismim [ism].)

Misollar:

  • What is your name? → My name is John.
  • What is your name? → My name is Sarah.

Savolning Qisqartirilgan Shakli

"What is your name?" savolining qisqartirilgan shakli: "What's your name?". Ma’nosi o‘zgarmaydi va rasmiy bo‘lmagan muloqotda ko‘proq ishlatiladi.

Misol:

  • What's your name? → My name is Tom.

Tavsiyalar

Ushbu savolni o‘rganishda va ishlatishda:

  • O‘zingizni tanishtirishni mashq qiling.
  • O‘rganilgan jumlalarni talaffuz bilan yodlang.
  • Suhbatda qanday javob berishni oldindan rejalang.

Mashqlar: "What is your name?"

1. Ingliz Tilida Savol Tuzing

Quyidagi jumlalar uchun savol tuzing:

  • My name is John. → _______
  • My name is Sarah. → _______

2. To‘g‘ri Javobni Tanlang

Quyidagi savollarga mos javobni tanlang:

  • What is your name? → My name is Jack. / I like coffee.
  • What's your name? → My name is Emma. / I go to school.

3. Savolni Tugating

Quyidagi savollardagi bo‘sh joylarni to‘ldiring:

  • What _______ your name?
  • _______ is your name?
  • What's _______ name?

4. Suhbatni To‘ldiring

Quyidagi suhbatdagi bo‘sh joylarni to‘ldiring:

A: _______ your name?
B: My name is Alex.
A: Nice to meet you, Alex.

5. Tarjima Qiling

Quyidagi gaplarni ingliz tiliga tarjima qiling:

  • Sizning ismingiz nima?
  • Mening ismim Tom.
  • Sizni tanishganimdan xursandman.

6. To‘g‘ri Shaklni Tanlang

Quyidagi gaplardan to‘g‘risini tanlang:

  • What is your name? / What are your name?
  • My name is Anna. / My names are Anna.

7. Suhbat Tuzing

Quyidagi iboralar yordamida qisqa dialog yozing:

What is your name? / My name is Emily. / Nice to meet you.

8. Bo‘sh Joylarni To‘ldiring

Quyidagi gaplarni mos so‘zlar bilan to‘ldiring:

  • _______ is your name?
  • My name _______ Jack.
  • Nice to _______ you.

9. Suhbatni Davom Ettiring

Quyidagi suhbatni davom ettiring:

A: What's your name?
B: My name is Alice.
A: _______

10. Qisqartirilgan Shaklni Ishlating

"What’s your name?" savolini ishlatib gap tuzing.

  • Misol: What's your name? → My name is Kate.
