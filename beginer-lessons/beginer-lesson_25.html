"What is your name?"

What Does "What is your name?" Mean?

"What is your name?" means "Sizning ismingiz nima?" in Uzbek. It is a simple and essential question commonly used during introductions. By answering this question, you can introduce yourself.

Structure of the Question

The question "What is your name?" is structured as follows:

  • What: Nima?
  • is: The present tense form of the verb "to be" (used with singular subjects).
  • your: Your.
  • name: Ism.

When put together: "What is your name?" → Sizning ismingiz nima?

How to Answer

To answer this question, use the following formula:

  • My name is [name]. (Mening ismim [ism].)

Examples:

  • What is your name? → My name is John.
  • What is your name? → My name is Sarah.

Shortened Form of the Question

The shortened form of "What is your name?" is: "What's your name?". Its meaning remains the same and is more commonly used in informal conversations.

Example:

  • What's your name? → My name is Tom.

Tips

To master this question and use it effectively:

  • Practice introducing yourself.
  • Memorize the sentences with proper pronunciation.
  • Plan your response ahead of time in conversations.

Mashqlar: "What is your name?"

1. Ingliz Tilida Savol Tuzing

Quyidagi jumlalar uchun savol tuzing:

  • My name is John. → What is your name?
  • My name is Sarah. → What is your name?

2. To‘g‘ri Javobni Tanlang

Quyidagi savollarga mos javobni tanlang:

  • What is your name? → My name is Jack. / I like coffee.
  • What's your name? → My name is Emma. / I go to school.

3. Savolni Tugating

Quyidagi savollardagi bo‘sh joylarni to‘ldiring:

  • What is your name?
  • What is your name?
  • What's your name?

4. Suhbatni To‘ldiring

Quyidagi suhbatdagi bo‘sh joylarni to‘ldiring:

A: What is your name?
B: My name is Alex.
A: Nice to meet you, Alex.

5. Tarjima Qiling

Quyidagi gaplarni ingliz tiliga tarjima qiling:

  • What is your name?
  • My name is Tom.
  • Nice to meet you.

6. To‘g‘ri Shaklni Tanlang

Quyidagi gaplardan to‘g‘risini tanlang:

  • What is your name? / What are your name?
  • My name is Anna. / My names are Anna.

7. Suhbat Tuzing

Quyidagi iboralar yordamida qisqa dialog yozing:

What is your name? / My name is Emily. / Nice to meet you.

8. Bo‘sh Joylarni To‘ldiring

Quyidagi gaplarni mos so‘zlar bilan to‘ldiring:

  • What is your name?
  • My name is Jack.
  • Nice to meet you.

9. Suhbatni Davom Ettiring

Quyidagi suhbatni davom ettiring:

A: What's your name?
B: My name is Alice.
A: Nice to meet you, Alice. What do you do?

10. Qisqartirilgan Shaklni Ishlating

"What’s your name?" savolini ishlatib gap tuzing.

  • What's your name? → My name is Kate.
