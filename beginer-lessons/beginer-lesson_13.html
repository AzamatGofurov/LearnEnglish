Exercises: Write Your Weekly Schedule

1. Fill in Daily Activities

Complete the sentences with the days of the week:

  • I go to the gym on _______. (e.g., Monday)
  • My English class is on _______.
  • We have family dinner every _______.

2. Arrange Activities by Day

Assign the following tasks to the appropriate days of the week:

  • Go shopping
  • Attend a meeting
  • Visit grandparents
  • Relax at home

3. Write Your Weekly Schedule

Write one activity for each day of the week:

  • Monday: _______
  • Tuesday: _______
  • Wednesday: _______

4. Choose the Correct Preposition

Add the correct prepositions to the following sentences:

  • I have a meeting _______ Tuesday. (e.g., on)
  • We always go for a walk _______ Sunday afternoon.
  • My yoga class starts _______ Friday morning.

5. Identify Free Days in the Weekly Schedule

Find the empty days in the following schedule:

Monday: Work
Tuesday: _______
Wednesday: Gym
Thursday: _______

8. Write About Your Busiest Day

Answer the following questions:

  • Which day is the busiest for you?
  • What do you usually do on that day?

9. Write Weekly Tasks

Write your weekly tasks matching them with the days of the week:

  • Shopping on _______
  • Studying English on _______
  • Relaxing on _______

10. Create a Dialogue

Write a short dialogue using the following prompts:

What do you usually do on Monday? / I go to work and have a meeting. / What about Tuesday?
