Savollarga Oddiy Javoblar

Savollarga Oddiy Javob Berish

Savollarga oddiy javoblar berish muloqotni sodda va tushunarli qilishga yordam beradi. Ingliz tilida savollarga qisqa javob berishda quyidagi qoida va jumlalar keng qo‘llaniladi.

"Yes" yoki "No" Javoblari

Ingliz tilida "yes" yoki "no" bilan javob berish eng oddiy va keng tarqalgan usuldir. Quyidagi misollarga e’tibor bering:

  • Do you like coffee? → Yes, I do. yoki No, I don’t.
  • Is he a teacher? → Yes, he is. yoki No, he isn’t.
  • Are they students? → Yes, they are. yoki No, they aren’t.

Jumlalar Bilan Oddiy Javoblar

Ba’zan savollarga jumlalar yordamida oddiy va tushunarli javoblar beriladi:

  • What is your name? → My name is Anna.
  • Where are you from? → I am from New York.
  • How old are you? → I am 25 years old.

Savol Turiga Qarab Javoblar

Har xil savollar turiga qarab javoblar quyidagicha bo‘lishi mumkin:

  • Who: Savol subyekt haqida. Who is she? → She is my sister.
  • What: Savol biror narsa haqida. What is this? → This is a book.
  • Where: Savol joy haqida. Where do you live? → I live in London.

Tavsiyalar

Savollarga oddiy javob berishni yaxshilash uchun:

  • Savollarga qisqa va aniq javob berishni mashq qiling.
  • Kunlik hayotda tez-tez uchraydigan savollar va javoblarni talaffuz qiling.
  • Do‘stlaringiz yoki o‘qituvchingiz bilan muloqot qilishda o‘rgangan javoblarni ishlating.

Mashqlar: Savollarga Oddiy Javoblar

1. "Yes" yoki "No" Bilan Javob Bering

Quyidagi savollarga "Yes" yoki "No" bilan javob bering:

  • Do you like tea?
  • Is she a doctor?
  • Are they students?

2. To‘g‘ri Javobni Tanlang

Quyidagi savollarga mos javobni tanlang:

  • Do you play football? → Yes, I do. / No, I don’t.
  • Is it raining? → Yes, it is. / No, it isn’t.
  • Are they your friends? → Yes, they are. / No, they aren’t.

3. Javobni Tugating

Quyidagi javoblardagi bo‘sh joylarni to‘ldiring:

  • Yes, _______ do.
  • No, _______ isn’t.
  • Yes, _______ are.

4. Savolga To‘liq Javob Bering

Quyidagi savollarga to‘liq javob yozing:

  • What is your name?
  • Where are you from?
  • How old are you?

5. Savollarga Mos Javobni Tanlang

Quyidagi savollarga mos javobni belgilang:

  • Who is she? → She is my sister. / She is a book.
  • What is this? → This is a pen. / This is running.
  • Where do you live? → I live in Paris. / I live at 6 PM.

6. Bo‘sh Joylarni To‘ldiring

Quyidagi javoblardagi bo‘sh joylarni mos so‘zlar bilan to‘ldiring:

  • Yes, I _______.
  • No, she _______.
  • Yes, they _______.

7. Tarjima Qiling

Quyidagi savollar va javoblarni ingliz tiliga tarjima qiling:

  • Siz qayerdansiz? – Men Toshkentdanman.
  • Bu nima? – Bu kitob.
  • Siz kitob o‘qiysizmi? – Ha, men o‘qiyman.

8. Qisqa Suhbat Tuzing

Quyidagi iboralardan foydalanib qisqa dialog yozing:

Do you like coffee? / Yes, I do. / What about tea? / No, I don’t.

9. Savollarni To‘ldiring

Quyidagi javoblarga mos savollar yozing:

  • Yes, I do. → _______
  • No, he isn’t. → _______
  • Yes, they are. → _______

10. Savollarga To‘g‘ri Javobni Yozing

Quyidagi savollarga to‘g‘ri javob yozing:

  • Do you like music?
  • Is he your friend?
  • Are they from London?
