Mashqlar: Sonlarni Tanib, Yozib Berish

1. Sonlarni Ingliz Tilida Yozing

Quyidagi sonlarni ingliz tilida yozing:

  • 7
  • 23
  • 58
  • 102
  • 1 234

2. Talaffuzni Tanlang

Quyidagi sonlarning to‘g‘ri talaffuzini tanlang:

  • 45 → forty-five / fourty-five
  • 100 → one hundred / one handred
  • 1 000 → one thousand / one thausand

3. To‘g‘ri Tartibni Belgilang

Quyidagi sonlarni kichikdan kattaga qarab tartiblang:

34, 78, 12, 90, 56

4. To‘g‘ri Yozilishini Belgilang

Quyidagi sonlarni to‘g‘ri yozing:

  • 56 → fifty-six / fifty-six
  • 98 → ninety-eight / ninety eight
  • 123 → one hundred twenty-three / one hundered twenty three

5. Sonlarni Gapda Yozing

Quyidagi sonlarni gaplarda yozing:

  • I have _______ books. (35)
  • There are _______ students in the class. (50)
  • The price is _______ dollars. (120)

6. Katta Sonlarni Yozing

Quyidagi katta sonlarni ingliz tilida yozing:

  • 1 567
  • 12 345
  • 123 456

7. Yuzliklarni To‘ldiring

Quyidagi yuzlik sonlarni to‘ldiring:

  • 123 → one _______ twenty-three
  • 567 → five _______ sixty-seven
  • 890 → eight _______ ninety

8. Chiziqcha Qo‘shing

Quyidagi sonlarni yozishda chiziqchani to‘g‘ri joylashtiring:

  • 56 → fifty six / fifty-six
  • 89 → eighty nine / eighty-nine
  • 123 → one hundred twenty three / one hundred twenty-three

9. Sonlarni Talaffuz Qiling

Quyidagi sonlarni talaffuz qilib mashq qiling:

  • 34
  • 567
  • 1 234

10. Gaplarni To‘ldiring

Quyidagi gaplarni mos sonlar bilan to‘ldiring:

  • The building is _______ meters tall. (100)
  • She is _______ years old. (25)
  • The team scored _______ goals. (3)
